ADEBILEJE AYOTUNDE MARVELLOUS


My name is Adebileje Ayotunde Marvellous, I was born on the 4th of June in the year 2006, I am from Idanre in Ondo state, Nigeria.


I schooled in Nigeria, my primary and secondary education level were both completed in Kingstep International School in Ilorin, Kwara state where I grew up. My primary education level was completed in the year 2015 and my secondary in the year 2021 respectively. I am currently a student in the University of Ilorin studying Mechanical engineering.
During my leisure times I love watching movies, listening to music, playing video games, reading, swimming and travelling. I detest lying, fighting and noise.

I first started my journey in web development in the year 2020 during the COVID19 pandemic, my first course was on edx coding for beginners where I was introduced to the web and how the internet works. In the course we learnt basic HTML. After that I signed up on Udemy online coding class where I furthered my knowledge in HTML and it was then I learnt about CSS, making tables, forms and the many likes. After that I was intoduced to Javascript when I started learning on w3Schools where I gathered basic knowledge about Javascript. Having done all that I didn't stop there, I continued and updated myself by signing up and learning on Altschoolafrica which just broadened my knowledge on HTML, CSS and Javascript. After that I have learnt on other platforms like Coursera and the many likes.


I can now code in HTML, CSS and functionality Javascript, I am open to learning new things and I know that my journey has just begun.
